DIG 245 Everyone ♥ Math

by Erina Lee and Henry Howell

You, the guy to the left, just splurged on an enormous meal at a fancy restaurant. You want to tip the waiter to the right for his incredible service. If you tip too much, you'll go broke but the waiter will be happy. If you tip too little, you'll have extra money left over but the waiter will cry himself to sleep that night. You have to tip just the right amount—20%—to satisfy both your wallet and the waiter.

What is 20% of $150?
